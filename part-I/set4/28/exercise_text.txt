Purpose of this exercise: design a small class returning substrings from lines.
Design a class Line having two members:

a member bool getLine() returning true if the line read from cin contains non-ws (spaces, tabs) characters;
a member std::string next() returning the next substring from the line retrieved by getLine() consisting of non-ws characters, and an empty string if no such substring is available anymore.
Hints:

make sure that at every call of next() the begin position of the next substring is already known, and that his begin position is equal to string::npos if there are no more substrings.
both members require few lines of code. There's no need for additional (private) member functions.
Submit all your sources of the class Line.
